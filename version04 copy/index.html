Yes, or at least be ready to launch one with us during the first month.

Do I need to share a demo of my game?
+

Yes — to understand your game and create content effectively, we need access to a playable build.

Is there a trial period?
+

Not officially, but if you’re not satisfied after the first month, we’ll offer a 50% refund — no hard feelings.

Do you manage paid ads?
+

Yes. Once we align on campaign goals and budget, we handle setup, optimization, and management.

Do you create trailers?
+

We create short-form videos for social media and Steam updates. For cinematic trailers, we recommend working with a specialized studio.

Do you only work with Steam games? What about mobile?
+

We specialize in PC/Steam games. At this time, we do not support mobile game marketing.

Do you offer funding for revenue share projects?
+

Yes — we can invest up to $50,000 in select titles under our revenue share model. If you're interested, submit your game for review.

What if I only have a few marketing needs right now?
+

No problem. You can sign up, make your requests, then pause or cancel your plan anytime. Come back when you're ready for more support — we're flexible.

How do I communicate and track progress?
+

Once you sign up, you’ll get access to a dedicated Notion page. There, you can share info, track tasks, view updates, and communicate directly with us — all in one place.
